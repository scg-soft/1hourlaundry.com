Tips for Self Service

Loading to Washer

  • Group your clothes by fabric type and color
  • Always check tags for special instructions
  • Load cloths in Washer
  • Pile clothes loosely in washer, leaving about 5" of space from the top
  • Over-loading the washer or adding too much soap can cause the machine to leak or spin longer than intended

Add Detergent

  • Add 2 tablespoons Detergent per medium load
  • Too much detergent leaves residue in clothing and causes washer malfunction
  • For best results, use 2 tablespoons of our high-efficiency detergent
  • Use pods, but DO NOT place them in the soap tray just toss them in the tub with your clothes

Remove from Washer

  • Remove Clothing from Washer.
  • Upon cycle completion, leave the front-load washer door open to help the drum dry quickly
  • Please DO NOT slam the machine doors or soap tray

Load Dryer

  • Load Dryer.
  • Give a gentle flick for each cloth for better drying
  • Clean the lint screen to ensure maximum air flow is achieved to dry quickly and save energy
  • One washer load requires one dryer load
